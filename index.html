What are the Great Lakes

The Great Lakes of North America consist of five lakes in total (Lake Erie, Lake Huron, Lake Michigan, Lake Ontario, and Lake Superior). The Great Lakes are the largest group of freshwater lakes in the world by total surface area and rank second in the world for containing the most fresh water (twenty-one percent).

Protecting the Great Lakes

The Great Lakes are a National Treasure that many people want to protect against pollution, invasive species, overfishing, and more. There is even a website Great Lakes Restoration, this website has recent news, projects, results, funding, and more about what is going on in the Great Lakes and plans to protect and revive it. That's why it is important to educate ourselves on what is happening and what we can do to help.
